2. **Mathematical language** — Markov chains, transition matrix, Chapman-Kolmogorov.
3. **Long-run behaviour** — stationary distribution, convergence, mixing time.
4. **Transient behaviour** — fundamental matrix, absorption, hitting times.
5. **Continuous time** — generator $Q$, $P(t) = e^{Qt}$, birth-death.
6. **Applied scenarios** — S1–S5 with analytical results + simulated trajectories.
7. **Experimental validation** — all seven experiments and figures.
8. **Budget bridge** — headcount distribution → salary distribution → total cost.
9. **Practical framework** — a 4-step process for any planner.
10. **Conclusion** — what changes when you treat headcount as a process.
